    About Me

    I’ve spent the last few years working closely with real users, real data, and the consequences of systems that don’t quite work the way they should.

    In my current role supporting NHS software, that has meant diagnosing SQL issues and fixing data inconsistencies to keeping services stable for customers that depend on them. It’s work that’s taught me to communicate clearly and stay calm when details matter.

    My background is in mathematics. I took a detour into data science during an internship, and now I’m building my Python and AI skills with intention. Because through supporting systems, I have realised I want to be able to build them.

    Why Work With Me

    The more I learned, the more I was drawn toward automation, better tools, and smarter workflows. That’s what I’m starting to create, and much of it lives here on this site.

    I care about how things work, and I care about why they matter. During that internship, I built forecasting models and worked directly with Python data pipelines. It stuck with me and felt meaningful. It confirmed that this is the kind of work I want to grow into… Understanding systems, working with data, and creating things that are actually useful.

    Now I’m building that path with focus. I’m strengthening my Python and AI foundations through personal studies and hands-on projects. This site reflects that: practical work, visible progress, and an honest look at how I think.

    What I bring is technical care, real-world perspective, and curiosity. I’m looking for a team where those qualities can go further.
